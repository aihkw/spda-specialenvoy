Land Development

Development initiatives for land development in the Southern Philippines

Project Overview

Strategic land development is fundamental to attracting investments, creating employment clusters, and organizing economic activities for maximum efficiency and sustainability. The Southern Philippines possesses extensive land resources suitable for industrial, commercial, and special economic zone development, yet much of this potential remains underutilized due to unclear land ownership, inadequate infrastructure, and fragmented development planning. Unlocking these land assets through planned development can catalyze regional economic transformation and position Mindanao as a competitive investment destination.

The SPDA Land Development Project focuses on transforming idle and underutilized lands into productive economic zones through strategic planning, infrastructure provision, and investor facilitation. This initiative supports the development of industrial estates, special economic zones, agro-industrial parks, and integrated townships that provide world-class facilities for manufacturing, logistics, business process outsourcing, and export-oriented industries. By consolidating land, providing ready-to-build sites with complete utilities, and offering investment incentives, the project significantly reduces barriers to business establishment and expansion.

Through collaboration with national economic zone authorities, local government units, landowners, and private developers, SPDA facilitates large-scale land development projects that create economic ecosystems where businesses can thrive. This project embodies SPDA's vision of fostering balanced growth by ensuring that land resources are developed sustainably, benefitting local communities through employment, technology transfer, and shared prosperity across the Southern Philippines.

Key Objectives

  • Develop 20,000+ hectares of serviced industrial land through establishment of industrial estates, special economic zones, and export processing zones
  • Create integrated agro-industrial parks that link agricultural production with processing facilities, value-adding industries, and export markets
  • Establish free ports and logistics hubs that leverage the region's strategic location for regional and international trade
  • Resolve land ownership issues through titling programs, idle land utilization mechanisms, and transparent land banking systems
  • Implement sustainable land use planning that balances economic development with environmental conservation and community welfare

Focus Areas

Industrial Estates and SEZs

Development of world-class industrial parks with complete utilities, modern facilities, and streamlined business processes attracting manufacturing and export industries.

Agro-Industrial Parks

Integrated zones linking farms to processing facilities, creating value chains that increase farmer incomes while developing food processing and export industries.

Logistics and Free Ports

Strategic development of port-adjacent industrial zones, bonded warehouses, and trade facilitation centers optimizing cargo movement and trade processing.

Mixed-Use Townships

Planned communities integrating residential, commercial, institutional, and recreational uses creating vibrant, sustainable urban centers.

Expected Impact

Investment Attraction

Facilitation of 400-500 billion pesos in private investments across manufacturing, agro-processing, logistics, and business services sectors creating export-competitive industries.

Employment Generation

Creation of 300,000+ direct jobs in industrial zones, logistics operations, and support services, with significant multiplier effects in local economies.

Economic Clustering

Development of industry clusters and value chains that enhance productivity, facilitate technology transfer, and position Southern Philippines as regional manufacturing hub.

Sustainable Development

Planned land use preventing urban sprawl, protecting agricultural lands and ecosystems, while ensuring development benefits are shared with local communities.
